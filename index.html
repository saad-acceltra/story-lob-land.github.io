How StoryLob Works

From initial concept to final storyboard in four simple steps

01

Create Your Project

Set up your project with a name, description, and style preferences. Choose your aspect ratio (3:2, 2:3, or 1:1) and configure your AI provider settings.

02

Design Your Characters

Create detailed character profiles and generate character images with AI. Refine appearances and maintain consistent character visuals throughout your story.

03

Organize Scenes & Frames

Structure your narrative with scenes and frames. Use the intuitive drag-and-drop interface to reorder and organize your story's visual flow.

04

Generate & Refine Images

Bring your story to life with AI-generated images for each frame. Iterate, refine, and perfect your visual narrative with our powerful version control system.
